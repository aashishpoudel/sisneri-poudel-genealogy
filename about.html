c) How are persons added?

The above book is treated as the authoritative source. Nothing is removed from the book’s data. For new entries, only verified sources are added. Any newly verified person will display a (+) plus sign next to his/her name.

d) Why do some people have sons, some only daughters, and some have a mix of sons/daughters?

This is all according to the book "Paudel-Kul-Prakash". A “mix” can appear when the book initially listed only daughters, and a verified son record was later obtained and added.

e) Will this sisneripoudel.com be updated with the upcoming third edition of "Poudel-Kul-Prakash"?

Of course.
